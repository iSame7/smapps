SnakeByte for Apple Watch

Smiple snake game for Apple Watch

You are a white square snake, All you have to do is control snake and eat as much as you can green squares. Each green square makes you longer. Be carful! while eating and moving on, don’t bite your own tail! User the arrow buttons to steer left or right.

control the snake by toching screen or using arrows.

Force Touch the Apple Watch screen to pause the game and choose to Resume or Restart.

Introducing the ability to change snake's skin color.
