TweetSpeak for iPhone

App to read tweets out loud.

TweetSpeak is an app that reads your Twitter feeds, this App to read tweets out loud. this app is for everyone who like to read tweets while driving, running, or biking. You can change speaking speed rate, also TweetSpeak can work even if it’s in background or the iPhone is locked.

Just one click and you will hear your tweets.

TweetSpeak can detect different languages e.g. English, Francia’s, Arabic, Spanish and more languages coming in next release.
